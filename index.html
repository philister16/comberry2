Comberry

How to use Comberry in 12 easy steps

0. Us the tabs at the bottom to navigate.

1. Use the 'Add New ...' button to bring up the input form.

2. Type your details in the form and tap 'Create'.

3. All your added items compared are shown underneath the bar chart.

4. Tap an item to change its values.

5. Tap on a specific bar to show its value and use the tabs at the bottom of the screen to toggle between Volume (quantity), Cost, Revenue and Profit.

6. Hit 'Show combined bar' to show a special bar combining all your items.

7. Tap menu button in top right corner to toggle between different analysis.

8. Chose 'Side by Side' from menu to show all compared items at same volume (quantity).

9. Tap 'Change volume' to adjust the compared volume (quantity).

10. Maximize profit shows the maximal possible profit from the existing items vs. the current situation.

11. Minimize costs shows the minimal possible cost from the existing items vs. the current situation.

12. Optimize volume shows the possible volume reduction to still achieve same profit as currently.
